A CRUNCH OUT OF BULLYING

• ADULT COMEDY

Sheridan Animation Thesis Film.

A young, obese, handicapped fan attempts to befriend a rude gang of fast-food mascot athletes at a friendly game of backetball.

A story designed with the intention of being risky with absurdist, crude humor, poking fun at the uncomfortable issues of bullying.

Inspirations: Ren & Stimpy, Smiling Friends, White Men Can't Jump, 90s Hip-Hop Music.
